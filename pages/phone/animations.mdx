## Enable Developer Options

1. Open settings and tap **About Phone**
2. Tap **Software Information**
3. Tap **Build Number** 7 times

## Speed Up System Animations

1. Open settings and select **Developer Options**
2. Scroll down to the **Drawing** section
3. Change the **Windows Animation Scale**, **Transition Animation Scale**, and **Animator Duration Scale** from 1x to 0.5x.

## Background Process Limit (after every restart of the phone)
1. Open settings and select **Developer Options**
2. Scroll all the way down to the option called **Background Process Limit** and set it to _at most 4 processes_
